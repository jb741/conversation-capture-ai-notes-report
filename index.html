Executive verdict

There is no single magic app. The winning answer is a 3-layer capture stack.

Real life has different capture surfaces: surprise in-person conversations, personal phone calls, and structured business calls. One tool will fail somewhere. The smallest reliable system uses the best tool for each surface.

Buy first

PLAUD Note Pro

Most practical dedicated recorder: discreet, mature AI notes, and better for real-world conversations than a phone app you might forget to open.

Avoid as first move

Visible AI pendants

Limitless is no longer sold to new customers; Bee/Omi/Friend-style devices feel more like lifelogging companions than polished business note capture.

The recommended capture stack

This gives you coverage without needing to change your whole life.

Layer 1

iPhone native recording

Use for: quick personal calls and emergency capture.

  • Built into iOS 18.1+ Phone app.
  • Stores recording/transcript in Notes.
  • Summary available with Apple Intelligence on supported devices.
Layer 2

PLAUD Note Pro

Use for: in-person meetings, hallway chats, lunches, site visits, and important calls.

  • Discreet card/phone style.
  • Meeting + phone-call modes.
  • AI summaries, templates, speaker labels, search.
Layer 3

Business phone system

Use for: SmartRide, Sign Tech, HCGC numbers.

  • Quo/OpenPhone for simplest shared inbox.
  • Dialpad for stronger AI intelligence.
  • Records only calls routed through business number/app.

Business phone layer

This is how conversations become searchable business memory instead of scattered personal recordings.

Simplest SMB pick

Quo / OpenPhone Business

Best first trial for SmartRide, Sign Tech, or Hub City if the goal is simple shared numbers, calls, texts, recordings, transcripts, summaries, and team contact history.

  • Approx. $23/user/mo annual Business tier.
  • Automatic recording + AI transcripts/summaries on Business/Scale.
  • Great small-team shared inbox.
Stronger AI pick

Dialpad Pro

Best if you want real-time transcription, call summaries, action items, searchable transcripts, analytics, coaching, and stronger call intelligence.

  • Approx. $25/user/mo annual Pro tier.
  • More powerful AI layer.
  • More setup/admin than Quo.
Important limitation

These systems generally record calls made/received through the business number/app. They usually do not record random personal cellular calls placed from the native iPhone dialer.

Consent and legality

Not legal advice. Practical operator rule: because Jon operates across PA, MD, VA, SC, and FL, use the strictest default.

Safe default

Assume all-party consent unless proven otherwise.

PA, MD, and FL are all-party consent states. VA and SC are one-party consent, but mixed-state calls make the stricter rule safer.

In-person script

“I use an AI recorder so I don’t miss details. Are you okay if I record and transcribe this for my notes?”

Phone script

“This call may be recorded and transcribed for notes, quality, and follow-up. Is that okay?”
